# Basic Filipino Greetings

## Vocabulary
* **Magandang umaga** - Good morning
* **Magandang hapon** - Good afternoon
* **Magandang gabi** - Good evening
* **Kumusta ka?** - How are you?

## Dialogue
**Person A**: Magandang umaga, Maria.
**Person B**: Magandang umaga din, Juan. Kumusta ka?
**Person A**: Mabuti naman. Salamat.

## Grammar Note
In Filipino, "din" or "rin" means "also" or "too". Use "rin" after vowels and "din" after consonants.
