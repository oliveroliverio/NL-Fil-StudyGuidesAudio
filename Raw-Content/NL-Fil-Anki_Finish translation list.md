### Gamit ng "Ano" at "Sino:" Ang Republika ng Pilipinas at mga Pambansang Sagisag



### Ano?

"Ano" which means "what" is one of the most common question words in Filipino. Here is an opportunity for you to practice using the word in questions and answers at the same time that you are able to learn or recall Philippine national symbols.

"Ano-ano" is used when asking for multiple answers.

- **Examples:**
  - Ano ang pangalan mo? *(What is your name?)*
  - Ano-ano ang mga kulay ng watawat ng Pilipinas? *(What are the colors of the Philippine flag?)*

"Sino" literally translates to "Who." It is the equivalent of "Ano" for names of persons. "Sino-sino" is the equivalent of "Ano-ano."

- **Examples:**
  - Sino si Emilio Aguinaldo? *(Who is Emilio Aguinaldo?)*
  - Sino-sino ang mga kaibigan mo? *(Who are your friends?)*

Please refer to page 12 of the textbook for the list of national symbols *(Mga Pambansang Sagisag)*.

**Mga Panghalip na “Ang”**

| Singular                         | Plural                                             |
|----------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| Ako (I)                          | Kami (We *exclusive. You are NOT in the group.*)   |
|                                  | Tayo (We *inclusive. You ARE IN the group!*)       |
| Ikaw (You)                       | Kayo (You)                                         |
| Siya (He, She, Personalized It)  | Sila (They)                                        |

### Expressing thanks, apology; Other Common Expressions



**Here are more expressions to remember:**

- **Salamat/ Maraming salamat** - Thanks; Thank you; Thank you so much.
- **Walang anuman** - You are welcome.
- **Paumanhin po** - Excuse me.
- **Makikiraan po** - May I pass through/pass by? [When passing through a crowd, or pass between people talking]
- **Patawad (po)** - I'm sorry.
- **Pasensiya na (po).** - I'm sorry.
- **Sori (po)** - I'm sorry.
- **OK lang./Wala 'yan** - That's alright.
- **Sandali lang** - Just a second.
- **Tayo na/Halina kayo** - Let's go.
- **Tuloy ka!/Tuloy po!** - Come in/Please come in!
- **Siyempre!** - Of course.
- **Huwag kang mag-alala** - Don't worry.
- **Sandali lang** - Wait!/Please wait.
- **Hindi [na] bale** - Never mind.
