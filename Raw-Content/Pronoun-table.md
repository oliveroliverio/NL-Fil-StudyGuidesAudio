# **Filipino Pronoun Table**

| **Person** | **Singular** | **Plural** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1st Person (Speaker)** | **ako** (I, me) | **tayo** (we *inclusive*: speaker + listener(s))**kami** (we *exclusive*: speaker + others, *not* listener) |
| **2nd Person (Listener)** | **ikaw / ka** (you) | **kayo** (you plural; also polite singular) |
| **3rd Person (Other)** | **siya** (he/she) | **sila** (they) |
